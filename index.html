Go Uptown

We are Go Uptown, a band that is just a group of misfits that love to play music. We consider ourselves a rock band, but we play a little bit of everything, whether that be pop, country, rap, or alternative.

There are no limits to the creativity that fuels our music. Our goal is to make music that is fun and enjoyable for everyone.
